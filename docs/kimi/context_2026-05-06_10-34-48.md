## Important Decisions & Corrections

1. **Aswas WhatsApp Images:** All 45 WhatsApp images (`WhatsApp Image 2026-02-27 at ...`) classified as new frozen ready-to-eat products.
2. **Malabar Choice "Copy of MALABAR":** 15 images with export-oriented packaging classified as NEW distinct products.
3. **Corrections Applied:**
   - Eastern Chick Peas 1kg → NEW
   - Eastern Cumin Seed 100g → NEW
   - Malabar Choice Jaggery Powder → matched to `MCJAGPOW`
   - Malabar Choice Cumin Whole → matched to `MCCUM`
   - Malabar Choice Cardamom → NEW
   - Viswas Whole Coriander → NEW
4. **Placeholder Detection:** Upload script checks for `image-392ae88a5edcfe8b29b5e84dffd03ba833be05e9-900x1200-jpg` and replaces it with real product images.
5. **AVIF Format Issue:** Sanity does not support `.avif` files. 19 MAGIC TASTE products failed upload due to this.
